  • About this Online Courses

    • The FOSSEE team at IIT Bombay offers free online courses in Python designed and curated by Prof.Prabhu Ramachandran(Dept. of Aerospace Engineering,IIT Bombay). These are interactive courses, Participants will gain hands-on experience in Python through a series of practice sessions and quizzes.

    • The course is spread on the span of 3 weeks, anyone interested to learn Python can take up the course irrespective of background(student/working professional).

    • While taking the course if you have any queries, you can post that on our FORUMS(Python section) which will be answered by FOSSEE's Python team.

    • Participants who meet the criteria mentioned will receive e-certificates within 15 working days from the day of course completion. Certificates will be issued on registered email address.

  • Instructions

    • Go to Yaksh, register and enroll for Basic Programming using Python by searching for the course using code: BPPY

    • After your enrollment is accepted, access "Enrolled Courses" tab you will be taken to the modules page, follow along the content sequentially.

    • You have to complete all the lessons, exercises and quizzes in a module to move to the next module.

    • You can revisit lessons for better understanding but quizzes can be attempted only once.

  • Duration

    • 3 weeks is the course window, you can devote 4 hours a day on weekend or 1 hour each day or as per your availability.

  • Benefits

    • The Course is absolutely free and a Certificate will be awarded on successful completion
